Why Out and About

Out and About is a small, intentional summer program run by a veteran educator out of a real home. Not a gymnasium. Not a waitlist. Not a screen. Just a small group of kids having the kind of summer they'll still be talking about when school starts.

Only 4 kids. Ever.

The Out and About Difference

Simple. Safe. Genuinely fun.

"Remember when summers felt like summers? Muddy, curious, unhurried. Kids who came home tired from actually doing things. That's what we're going for."

This isn't institutional summer care. There are no lanyards, no color-coded groups, no identical days. It's a small home, a caring adult, and a handful of kids who get to just be kids — with real adventures built in. We might even learn something along the way. Don't tell the kids.
